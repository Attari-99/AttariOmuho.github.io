My name is Felix Attari Omuho. I am a product and industrial designer, illustrator and motion designer from South Sudan: the first of the kind.

Have you ever wondered what African art and design will look like in the next decade? Have you stopped to think that what was stolen from Africa decades ago were the physical manifestations of something much greater inside of us?

The artifacts made by the generations before are what were stolen. But how does that translate to me not carrying the creativity, spirit and soul of these artifacts forward? Why would I whine over spilled milk? I have all the tools I need with me to create something new and - possibly - even better: my soul, my story, my experiences, my culture, my people,, my country... MY BEING.

"Create your own reality" Life, for me, is a continuous process of creating my own reality WITH anyone and everyone.


Let's create then, shall we?
